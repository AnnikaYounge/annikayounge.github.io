**about me**

I'm graduating from Stanford with a *B.S. Mathematical and Model Engineering*, and will be starting as a PhD student in Stanford's Department of *Management Science and Engineering* in fall 2026. I'm interested in structure and uncertainty in (social) science, especially methods in causal inference, topological and spectral analysis, active learning, networks, and model uncertainty.

I'm currently focused on research, graduation requirements, and humanitarian and global health work. See [current_favs](current_favs.md) for assorted thoughts and reads!
