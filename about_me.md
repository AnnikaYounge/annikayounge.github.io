**about me**

I'm starting a PhD at Stanford's Department of *Management Science and Engineering*. I'm graduating from Stanford with a *B.S. Mathematical and Model Engineering* (individually-designed major in Engineering). I'm interested in structure and uncertainty in (social) science, especially topics in causal inference, topological and spectral analysis, active learning, networks, and model uncertainty.

I'm currently focused on research, graduation requirements, and humanitarian and global health work. See [current_favs](current_favs.md) for assorted thoughts and reads!

**featured projects**
* Learning and dynamics of belief under model uncertainty
* Causality in pediatric training and hospital systems strengthening
* Geometric approaches to hypothesis generation and model discovery
* Heterogeneity and structure in reinforcement learning
* Reading group (cartography, spatial analysis, time and imagination, epistemics of abstraction and design)
* Skepticism in social learning and implications for political polarization, inequality, and persuasion
